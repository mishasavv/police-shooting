Police Shootings

This interactive map shows crowdsourced data from deadspin regarding police shootings in the US. Each clickable point on the map corresponds to a reported shooting.

Bright red points show incedents where the victim was unarmed, and darker maroon points correspond to victims being armed.

Use the checkboxes to display the points, which are catagorized by race of victim
